🧠 Brain Hemorrhage

A brain hemorrhage is a type of stroke caused by an artery bursting in the brain, leading to localized bleeding in surrounding tissues.

⚠️ Causes

  • 💉 High blood pressure
  • 🚑 Head trauma or injury
  • 🩸 Aneurysms (weakened blood vessels)
  • 🧬 Blood vessel abnormalities
  • 💊 Excessive use of blood thinners

🚨 Symptoms

  • 🤕 Sudden severe headaches
  • 💪 Weakness or numbness in limbs
  • 👀 Blurred or double vision
  • 🤢 Nausea and vomiting
  • 🗣️ Difficulty speaking or understanding speech
  • 🌀 Dizziness or loss of balance
